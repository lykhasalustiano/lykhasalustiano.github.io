HOBBIES AND SKILLS

I have plenty of hobbies and skills that I've grown fond of over time. As I recognized some of them as abilities, I took it upon myself to learn more and sharpen these skills.

WATCHING

I have a deep interest in watching various types of series, including anime, C-dramas, K-dramas, and J-dramas. I particularly enjoy live-action adaptations of anime, as they bring a unique perspective to the stories I love. In addition to these, I also have a fondness for cartoons, which adds to my diverse entertainment tastes.

READING

Growing up, I was captivated by fantasy books for kids and novels like Harry Potter by J.K. Rowling. My interests in horror, sci-fi, fiction, and romance shaped my reading habits and inspired me to write my own stories. These genres fueled my imagination and passion for storytelling, leading me to craft my own narratives.

DRAWING

This ability runs in the family on my father's side. While I may not be as skilled as my uncle, who is especially talented, I have improved significantly over the years through practice. I focus on drawing cartoons, anime, and 3D portraits, and I'm always working to enhance my skills. I also put effort into refining my grammar and expanding my writing, aiming to create longer and more engaging pieces.

WRITING

I love writing, especially fan fiction. For example, I sometimes create my own characters within a series I enjoy, making it feel like I'm part of the story. I also write my own stories and publish them on Wattpad, with 6 years of experience in creating fictional, fan-made content purely for fun.
